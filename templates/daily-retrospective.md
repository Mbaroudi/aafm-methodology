## Facilitator Tips

### For Flow Masters / Scrum Masters:

**Create Psychological Safety**:
âœ… Emphasize "no blame" culture
âœ… Focus on process, not people
âœ… Celebrate failures as learning opportunities
âœ… Encourage quiet voices to speak
âœ… Thank people for honesty

**Keep Energy High**:
âœ… Use timers to maintain pace
âœ… Vary retrospective formats (avoid monotony)
âœ… Celebrate wins enthusiastically
âœ… End on positive note (actions = hope)

**Drive to Action**:
âœ… Every retrospective must produce actions
âœ… Actions must be specific and achievable
âœ… Follow up on previous actions first
âœ… Track completion rate
âœ… Hold team accountable (gently)

**Leverage AI**:
âœ… Let AI prepare data (saves time)
âœ… Use AI insights to guide discussion
âœ… Have AI suggest root causes

ğŸš© Blame culture ("Why did X do that?")

ğŸš© Running long (>30 min = losing focus)

**If you see red flags**:

2. Change format to re-engage
3. Have 1-on-1 conversations to understand blockers
4. Consider bringing in external facilitator
5. Revisit team working agreements

## Special Retrospectives

### Weekly Deep Dive (Once per week, 60 min)

In addition to daily retrospectives, consider a weekly deep dive:

```
Agenda:
1. Week in review (15 min)
   - Outcomes delivered: 5/5
   - Velocity trends
   - Quality trends

2. Deep discussion (30 min)
   - Pick one topic to go deep on
   - Root cause analysis
   - Multiple solutions brainstorming

3. Process improvements (15 min)
   - Methodology adjustments
   - Tool evaluations
   - Team capability building
```

### Monthly Team Health Check (90 min)

```
Agenda:
1. Team satisfaction survey results
2. Burnout check (workload, stress levels)
3. Skill development discussions
4. Career growth conversations
```

## Retrospective Checklist

**Before Retrospective**:
- [ ] AI Scribe prepared metrics
- [ ] Room/call ready
- [ ] Timeboxes set
- [ ] Previous actions reviewed

**During Retrospective**:
- [ ] Everyone participates
- [ ] Data reviewed
- [ ] All 4Ls covered (or chosen format)
- [ ] 2-3 actions committed
- [ ] Actions are specific and owned

**After Retrospective**:
- [ ] AI Scribe documents outcomes
- [ ] Actions added to today's work
- [ ] Summary shared with team
- [ ] Retrospective insights added to knowledge base

---

**Remember**: Retrospectives drive continuous improvement. Without them, you're repeating mistakes. With them, you're compounding learning.
